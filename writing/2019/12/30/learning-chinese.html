How to Learn Chinese

Imagine yourself as a 20 something, English native speaker, with a long term vision of speaking, writing, and painting Chinese for no particular reason. Imagine yourself as me, basically.

I have noticed that learning Chinese as someone of my demographic is intimidating and haphazard.

I have tried

  • a weekly in-person class
  • several language learning apps, including Duolingo
  • downloading Chinese keyboard

and have ended up

  • stopping showing around week 5 of 10
  • deleting the apps after finding them too difficult or too easy. Duolingo was especially dramatic.
  • keeping Chinese keyboard despite everything and the fact that it screws with emoji keyboard access
